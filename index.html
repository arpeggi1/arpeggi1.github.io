About Me

Hi, I'm Arpeggi! I'm a Radiohead enthusiast, hardware student, and I'm from Portugal.

I enjoy creating fun projects and learning new things. I'm starting this website to share my personal projects—some for fun, some with real utility for others.

I'm a big fan of free and open source software, and I love experimenting with hardware. My mission is to make things that everyone can enjoy, even on older devices.

I also make music and hope you'll check it out. Most of my playlists are on Spotify, so give them a listen!
